About Daniel Wanjala

From growing up in Kenya and witnessing systemic inefficiencies firsthand, I've dedicated my career to using data science and machine learning as tools for economic transformation and social impact.

Currently serving as Senior IT Manager and Data Scientist at Kingdom Hospital, I bridge the gap between technology infrastructure and data-driven healthcare solutions. My journey from self-taught coder to professional data scientist has been driven by one question: "How can we use technology to create systems that serve people better?"

I'm pursuing an MSc in Data Science at Cooperative University of Kenya while continuing to develop predictive models for healthcare analytics, economic forecasting, and social impact measurement.
